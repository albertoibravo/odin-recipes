Tuna Salad

Virgina's tuna salad was always a summer Saturday favorite for my grandparents and me. Great served on a large lettuce leaf. You may also add grapes or chopped apples if you wish.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • can tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • stalk celery

Steps

  1. Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water
  2. Mix together tuna and mayonnaise in a medium bowl.
  3. Remove egg from hot water; cool for 5 minutes
